# Standardization
The mathematical equation to standardise a [[Distribution]]
$$z = \frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}$$
## Purpose
Each value of $x$ is placed on the same scale with [[Mean]] of 0 and [[Standard deviation]] of 1.
